Overview

Led creative production and editorial development of Paramount's first Streaming Technology Showcase — a digital hub designed to spotlight innovation across Paramount+ and Pluto TV.

Scope & Impact

The showcase highlighted recent advances in product design, engineering, and AI-driven innovation, illustrating how Paramount harnesses emerging technology to deliver world-class streaming experiences for over 100 million fans globally. Through strategic storytelling, content design, and cross-functional collaboration, the site positioned Paramount Streaming as a driver of creativity, technical excellence, and the future of entertainment.

Featured Innovations
Scene Detection

Emerging AI tools for scene detection and adaptive clipping enable smarter, faster storytelling at scale. Instantly surface the moments that matter most.

Daypart Personalization

From morning news to late-night drama, Paramount's dayparting engine dynamically adapts what viewers see — serving the right story at exactly the right moment.

Short Form, Big Impact

Millions of iconic moments from Paramount's extensive catalog — personalized for browsing. From plot twists to sports highlights to breaking news, viewers can sample before diving into their next obsession.
